XJTLU Library (https://lib.xjtlu.edu.cn) offers various e-resources accessible online via the Library website. The website also integrates many valuable tools, including DISCOVER and LibGuides, to help you better harvest academic resources.
LIBRARY CATALOGUE
Library Catalogue, also known as OPAC (Online Public Access Catalogue https://opac.xjtlu.edu.cn), allows everyone to publicly search and locate the Library’s print books, CDs/DVDs and subscribed e-books. More services will be experienced after logging in to “My Lib”, such as Patron Suggestion, Book Renewal, Book Reservation and Inter-Branch Delivery, etc.
